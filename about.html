About Blizzity

Empowering creators with powerful, private, and open-source media automation tools

Our Mission

Blizzity exists to democratize media automation. We believe that powerful workflow tools shouldn't require expensive software or compromise your privacy unnecessarily.

We're building a powerful, partially open-source platform that puts creators first. With flexible subscription plans and ongoing open-source development, we're creating the future of media automation.
